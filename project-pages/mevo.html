Project Details / Background
Working as a fleet manager for the Wellington based startup Mevo, resourcefulness and flexibility were crucial. Managing a small team I was responsible for maintaining the fleet including; utilisation optimisation, incident callouts, servicing and cleaning the vehicles to maintain a premium offering. My role then expanded to maintaining the electronic charging network to better utilise my electronics skill set. Management taught me how to direct and work well with the team while maintaining customer and staff satisfaction.
