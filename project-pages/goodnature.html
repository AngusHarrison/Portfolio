Project Details / Background
Working with the team at Goodnature I developed a strong foundation in Product development. This involved conceptualization, research, and refinement of hardware collaborating with Industrial and Mechanical faculty to develop the next generation of products for the ever changing market. Hardware and Firmware was developed using ESP and NRF chipsets to prototype and test PCBs. My workshop skills were honed as hardware was tested and integrated into the physical system. Learning reports and Engineering documentation was produced.
